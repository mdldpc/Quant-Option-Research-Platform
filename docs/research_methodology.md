# Quant Option Research Platform

## Research Methodology (V2)

### Purpose

The objective of Version 2 is to transform the existing quantitative research platform into a systematic research framework. Every study should answer a clearly defined research question and produce statistically supported conclusions.

---

## Standard Research Workflow

Every research study should follow the same workflow.

### Step 1 — Research Question

Clearly define the question being investigated.

Example:

* What is the empirical distribution of daily ATM implied volatility?
* Does the ATM term structure exhibit persistent contango?
* Does Signal Score contain predictive information?

---

### Step 2 — Dataset Description

Describe:

* Data source
* Sample period
* Sample size
* Variables used

---

### Step 3 — Descriptive Statistics

Calculate:

* Mean
* Median
* Standard deviation
* Quantiles
* Minimum
* Maximum
* Skewness
* Kurtosis

---

### Step 4 — Statistical Analysis

Depending on the study:

* Normality test
* Outlier detection
* Correlation analysis
* Regression analysis
* Hypothesis testing

---

### Step 5 — Visualization

Generate figures that support the statistical findings.

Typical figures include:

* Histogram
* Box plot
* Scatter plot
* Heatmap
* Time series
* QQ plot

---

### Step 6 — Interpretation

Interpret the statistical evidence in financial and economic terms.

Avoid describing figures only.

Instead, explain what the results imply.

---

### Step 7 — Research Conclusion

Summarize:

* Main findings
* Limitations
* Future extensions

---

## Guiding Principles

* Research questions drive the analysis.
* Statistics support conclusions.
* Figures illustrate evidence.
* Every conclusion must be reproducible.
* Code serves research, not the other way around.
